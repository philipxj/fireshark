Features

Weather information without opening a full website.

HK Weather Alerts keeps essential Hong Kong weather information close to your browser toolbar.

01

Warning badge

Show active weather warnings or current temperature on the browser action badge, with notification options for warning updates.

02

Weather at a glance

View current weather, special weather tips, forecast information, and compact daily outlooks in one popup.

03

Radar and lightning

Open radar imagery, lightning location, and related public weather resources from Hong Kong Observatory data sources.

Data Sources

Powered by public Hong Kong weather data.

Weather observations, forecasts, warnings, radar images, lightning information, and related resources are fetched from public data sources at runtime.

  • Hong Kong Observatory Open Data
  • Hong Kong Observatory website resources
  • DATA.GOV.HK open data terms
